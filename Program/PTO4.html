Simple Investment Calculator A simple investment calculator that takes an initial investment amount in dollars. Then accepts an interest rate between 2-7%.
Enter a investment term(ie. how long). Then specify that the term will be in years or months.
Then takes an additional amount of money for each term of the investment.
Result will be your total interest earned and the total amount in your account.
